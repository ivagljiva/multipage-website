Teaching

I enjoy teaching and am hoping to become a professor at a research university. I've been a computer science teaching assistant at Illinois Tech for over 3 years now. The introductory courses that I TA for are programming classes, so I spend a lot of time reviewing fundamental programming concepts with my students. I create my own review lectures, both for the practice of designing classes and for the opportunity to inject some fun examples into the students' curriculum. With every class I teach, I hope to get at least a few more students excited about coding.
